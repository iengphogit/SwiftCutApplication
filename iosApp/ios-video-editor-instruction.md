# iOS Video Editor Instruction

## Platform Choice
- iOS (best performance)

## Stack
- Swift + AVFoundation (UIKit or SwiftUI + UIKit)
- Use UIKit for the timeline (gestures, scrolling, hit-testing)
- Use SwiftUI for panels/settings if you want
- Playback/export with AVFoundation
- Heavy effects: Metal (optional, later)

## Best Fit
- CapCut-like smoothness and Apple-native exporting
